# Excledge ERP Product Brochure

## Product overview

Excledge ERP is a browser-based inventory and business management system designed to help small and medium-sized businesses manage stock, sales, purchasing, users, branches, subscriptions, and operational reporting from a single platform.

The platform combines front-office and back-office workflows in one application:
- stock and batch management
- point of sale and invoice generation
- customer and supplier management
- purchase orders and supplier payments
- branch-based operations
- debt tracking and repayment follow-up
- activity logging, notifications, and dashboard reporting
- Rwanda-focused EBM/VSDC integration readiness

## Target users

Excledge ERP is suitable for organizations that need structured inventory and sales control, especially:
- retail shops
- grocery stores
- hardware stores
- electronics stores
- clothing stores
- restaurants and bakeries
- other branch-based trading businesses

## Business value

Excledge ERP helps organizations:
- reduce stock loss and stock-outs through real-time inventory visibility
- improve sales control using POS workflows and receipt generation
- monitor product expiry, low-stock items, and stock movements
- manage customers, debts, and follow-up collections more effectively
- coordinate multiple branches under one organization
- improve accountability with role-based access and activity logs
- support subscription-based service delivery and centralized administration

## Key capabilities

### 1. Inventory and stock control
- product creation and updates
- barcode-ready product catalog structure
- batch tracking and expiry monitoring
- stock movement history and inventory ledger
- low-stock and expired product visibility
- stock transfers between branches
- warehouse support and branch-level stock views

### 2. Sales and point of sale
- fast POS workflow for daily sales
- support for cash, insurance, debt, mixed, card, and mobile money payment flows
- sale details, sale history, and receipt printing
- refund and cancellation support
- invoice numbering and audit trail

### 3. Customer and supplier management
- customer registration and debt balance tracking
- supplier records and supplier order workflows
- purchase order creation and status tracking
- supplier payment recording

### 4. Reporting and oversight
- dashboard KPIs and sales trend tracking
- sales reports
- inventory reports
- stock movement reports
- cash flow and debt payment reports
- organization-level and system-owner analytics

### 5. Multi-tenant and multi-branch support
- organization creation and switching
- branch-level access and operational scoping
- user invitations and role assignment
- subscription plan management per organization

### 6. Compliance and integration readiness
- tax category support at product level
- invoice sequencing and EBM transaction records
- support for Rwanda Revenue Authority EBM/VSDC integration workflows
- payment gateway integration support for subscription collection

## Technology profile

Excledge ERP is implemented as a modern web application using:
- React and TypeScript for the frontend
- Node.js and Express for the backend API
- PostgreSQL as the primary database
- Prisma ORM for data access and migrations
- Socket.IO for real-time notification delivery

## Deployment model

Excledge ERP is delivered as a web-based solution and can be deployed in a managed hosting or self-hosted server environment, depending on the customer implementation model. Users access the system through a browser on desktop or mobile devices with internet or approved network connectivity.

## Support model

Implementation support may include:
- installation and environment setup
- initial data configuration
- user onboarding and training
- subscription activation
- remote troubleshooting and maintenance

Support terms should be defined in the commercial agreement or service-level agreement.

## Optional integrations

- Rwanda EBM/VSDC submission services
